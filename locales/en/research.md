## Research

Our laboratory conducts research in the following areas:

### Nonlinear Physics

- **Quantum Chaos**: Analysis of chaotic behavior in quantum systems and its applications
- **Complex Fluids**: Numerical simulation and analysis of complex fluid phenomena
- **Musical Instrument Sound Production Mechanisms**: Research on acoustic characteristics and sound production mechanisms of musical instruments

### Artificial Intelligence / Life

- **Time Series Analysis**: Development of time series data analysis methods and prediction models
- **Swarm Dynamics**: Research on movement patterns of biological populations and swarm intelligence

### High-Performance Computing

- **Time-Parallelization**: Acceleration through time-parallelization methods
- **Efficiency Improvement through One-Sided Communication**: Methods for improving parallel computing efficiency using one-sided communication

### Computing

- **High-Speed Computing**: Optimization and acceleration of computational algorithms
- **Ultra-Parallel Computing**: Utilization and optimization of large-scale parallel computing environments
- **Real-Time Visualization**: Real-time visualization technology for computational results
- **Bio-Inspired Algorithms**: Development of algorithms inspired by biological behavior and evolution

### xReality

- **VR・MR・AR Applications**: Applied research on virtual reality, mixed reality, and augmented reality technologies
- **Image Processing**: Development and application of image and video processing technologies
